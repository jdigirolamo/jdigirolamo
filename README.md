## What I Build

I'm building **[TradeForge](https://github.com/jdigirolamo/tradeforge-ai-proxy)** — a full-stack AI-powered CRM for HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and general contracting businesses in Calgary and Alberta. It handles lead tracking, missed-call SMS follow-up, job pipeline management, and AI-assisted quoting.

By day I'm a **Building Operator Level 2 at the City of Calgary** (since 2016), maintaining recreation facilities across the city. That hands-on trades background is exactly why I'm building TradeForge — I know the industry from the inside.

## Featured Projects

### 🔧 [TradeForge AI Proxy](https://github.com/jdigirolamo/tradeforge-ai-proxy)
Serverless AI proxy layer on Cloudflare Workers + D1. Routes and logs all AI model API calls (Claude, OpenAI) for the TradeForge CRM — zero client-side credential exposure.

### ⚡ [TradeForge Workflow Engine](https://github.com/jdigirolamo/workflows-starter-template)
Real-time durable automation engine using Cloudflare Workflows, Durable Objects, and WebSockets. Powers lead follow-up sequences, appointment reminders, and job status notifications.
